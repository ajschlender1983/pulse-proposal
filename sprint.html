01

The frame

Every dollar answers one question.

Current blended CAC is $175. Every dollar this sprint spends — retainer, event, UGC creators, partnership outreach — has to beat what that same dollar would produce on Meta or Reddit at $175 per acquired customer. If it does not, we do not spend it.

Reported weekly. Every activity accountable to the benchmark.

The math

$175 in ads = 1 customer
$3,000 sprint retainer ≈ 17 customers
Month-1 target > 17 equivalents

Delivered through partnerships, referral infrastructure, event sales, and UGC that compound past a linear ad spend.

02

The lever

Ads are linear. Partnerships and referral compound.

Paid ads

$175 spent → 1 customer. Linear. Ceiling at spend budget.

Partnerships

1 signed engagement (Insight Timer's 10,000-user pilot) → hundreds of customers. Compounds.

Referral program

Infrastructure once, then $0 CAC on every referral. Grows with the installed base.

Event + UGC

One event = direct sales + fresh testimonials + partnership warming. Multi-purpose spend.

Customer education and success drive people into the referral program. That flywheel does not exist on paid channels.
