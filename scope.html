The backstory — how this comes together▾

Patrick Kronfli is a dear friend from Austin. He co-runs Unify.org and hosts both the Welcome Ceremony and the Eclipse Ceremony at Iceland Eclipse. I'm already in contact with him about a low-cost activation.

Unify.org has been coordinating synchronized global meditations for decades — one of the inspirations behind how I think about ritual, reflection, and shared consciousness. Their audience and Pulse's overlap heavily.

The theme fits perfectly. An eclipse is a ring — a black disc encircled by radiant light. Pulse is a ring worn on the finger to reflect and regulate. Same visual, same reflection-point framing, different scale. This is a launch moment that doesn't feel like a launch.

Half a dozen speakers and confirmed attendees are one degree from my network. Warm intros to potential investors and Insight Timer / Mindvalley-tier partners happen naturally over a shared meal — I don't have to manufacture the room.

The scarcity is real. Celestial Voyager passes are already sold out. The festival is capped at 3,333. The next Icelandic total eclipse after this one is in 2196. Every asset we capture becomes durable content for the priced-round preempt and every channel that comes after it.
