10 EBI design passes — what changed and why▾
EBI 01 · Open on the truth
Keep the doomscroll cold-open.
Considered opening on the ring gesture and flashing back. Rejected — too on-the-nose. The doomscroll earns the interruption. No title card. Trust the image.
EBI 02 · Make the interruption tactile
Hand-touches-ring, not CG halo.
Original brief implied the pulse. Better: a visible tell — the other hand touches the ring instinctively. Physical, not mystical. No visual FX on the ring itself.
EBI 03 · Earn "three minutes later"
Stay with the doomscroller through daylight.
The critique named this shot specifically. Same person, same bed, still there — and then the morning after. No cast swap in Act 1. The credible delta is one person, one arc.
EBI 04 · Cast for vibe
Plurality with personal style.
Age, body, skin, style plurality — but every cast member has a specific taste. Not "diverse extras." The 64-year-old is cool. The teen is cool. Different codes.
EBI 05 · Phones are partners
Show intentional phone use.
Every original shot showed the phone going away. Better: include Shot 05 — checking the weather. The ring changes the relationship. It doesn't demonize the device.
EBI 06 · Compress to 3 acts
Interruption. Habitat. Loop.
Original 12-shots-at-7s-each read as an ambient reel. Reorganized as Interruption (0–30) · Habitat (30–60) · Loop (60–90). Hits harder. Easier to cut down to 30s.
EBI 07 · Score the ambient, name it
Low frequencies, no bells.
Reference: Nils Frahm-adjacent, Nick Hakim, textural Bon Iver. Warmth in the low end. No singing bowls. No breath tracks. No handpan.
EBI 08 · One line at the end
"The off-switch you wear."
Pulled from the investor overview. One line only. Specs (21-day battery, screen-free, titanium) live in the 30s cut-downs. The film delivers the promise, not the spec sheet.
EBI 09 · Give the ring a hero moment
Slow-mo macro in Act 2.
Shot 10 is the reward — 3 seconds, titanium texture, the pulse firing. Product porn, but earned by the emotional runway before it.
EBI 10 · Close where we opened
The bedroom, at 8:47pm.
End on a lock screen reading 8:47pm. Same room, different time — the customer chose the evening this time. Closes the loop the doomscroller opened.
